SOS Page

The 5-Step Calming Flow:
1. Breathing: Follow the visual guide to slow down your breath (Inhale 4s, Hold 7s, Exhale 8s).
2. Short Chant Audio: Listen to the soothing Navkar Mantra to shift your focus.
3. Grounding: Simple physical techniques to reconnect with the present moment.
4. Reassurance: Affirmations to remind you that you are safe and supported.
5. Continue with Coach: Seamlessly transition to a chat with the AI for further support.

About Jain Vibes Page

Our Mission:
To make the profound wisdom of Jainism accessible and relevant to the mental health needs of the modern generation.

Why Jain Spiritual Practices Support Mental Peace:
Jainism offers practical tools for emotional regulation. Concepts like Anekantavada (many-sidedness) help reduce conflict, while meditation (Dhyana) fosters inner silence.

Blending Ancient Principles with Modern Wellness:
We bridge the gap between tradition and technology, offering a platform that respects your heritage while using advanced AI to provide real-time support.
